Design Philosophy

◈

Sealed Enclosure

No port. No chuffing. No tuning compromises. The sealed cabinet provides tight, articulate bass with a gentle 12dB/octave rolloff that integrates beautifully with room acoustics and subwoofers alike.

◈

Paper Cone Woofer

We chose paper for its natural damping properties and organic character. No synthetic coatings here — just honest, vintage-inspired warmth that makes guitars growl and pianos sing.

◈

Silk Dome Tweeter

A carefully selected 1" silk dome delivers extended highs without the clinical edge of metal domes. Cymbals shimmer, vocals breathe, and hours of listening remain fatigue-free.

◈

Hand-Tuned Crossover

Each crossover is assembled and voiced by ear over dozens of iterations. We prioritize phase coherence and transient response over flat frequency response — because music isn't measured in anechoic chambers.

"The S1-2 isn't designed to disappear. It's designed to be present — a physical artifact that demands attention before you even press play."
